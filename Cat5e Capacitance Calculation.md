# Cat5e Capacitance Calculation

$$C = \epsilon_0 \cdot \epsilon_r \cdot \frac{A}{d}$$
**Given**
 - Diameter of each coper cable is $0.5 mm$ or $0.0005 m$
 - Distance between the places (dielectric thickness) is $0.2 mm$ or $0.0002m$

**Area Calculation**
$$A = \pi (\frac{d}{2})^2$$
$$A = \pi (\frac{0.0}{2})^2$$
